The Austin Bullets are well renowned strategists, team players, and well disciplined kids.We have a cricket diverse team of spinners, batsmen, and much more.The team consists of players 7-12 years old.The team started with the kids passion for cricket.The parents helped them in many ways including transportation, utilities, money, and coaching.This team has some growing players and a growing community.Cricket wasn't well known in Austin but a group of parents,Independent Parent Initiative(IPI), bringing more cricket to Austin.These were the first steps into building a great team.(Jayant Veldanda,1)
